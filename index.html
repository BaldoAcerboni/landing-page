Spiders in my garage

One day I noticed a bunch of spiderwebs inside of my garage. And when I went to check them, I found quite a few dozens of the little fellas. On the side is the chonkiest of them all. I named her Big Mama.

Every time you're about to kill a spider, remember they eat flies and mosquitos, and to be honest I can not think of anything more positive any wild animal can do.
Furthermore consider that they use their venom for hunting, so they won't ever bite you, unless they are being squashed.
